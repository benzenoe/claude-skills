# Blog Post Generator for Eytan.com

Generate a professional blog post in Eytan Benzeno's voice on the topic: $ARGUMENTS

## Your Task

Create a complete, publication-ready blog post following these steps:

### Step 1: Deep Research

Use WebSearch to conduct comprehensive research on the topic:
- Search for recent news, trends, and developments (2024-2026)
- Find statistics, data points, and expert opinions
- Look for case studies and real-world examples
- Research any relevant industry implications

Perform at least 3-5 different searches to gather diverse perspectives.

### Step 2: Write the Blog Post

Write the blog post in **Eytan Benzeno's voice** with these characteristics:

**Voice & Tone:**
- Confident, direct, and authoritative
- Action-oriented with practical takeaways
- Balance personal insights with research/data
- Thought leadership style - strategic and forward-thinking
- Speak to entrepreneurs, business owners, real estate agents, and professionals
- Use "I" statements when sharing perspective
- Occasionally reference relevant businesses: REIGNation (coaching/mastermind), SoldHere (real estate), Bogen.ai (AI consulting)

**Structure (MUST FOLLOW):**
```markdown
[Opening hook - bold statement or question that grabs attention]

---

## [First Major Section]

[Content with insights, data, personal perspective]

---

## [Second Major Section]

[Content with bullet points where appropriate]

### [Subsection if needed]

[More detailed content]

---

## [Continue pattern for 4-6 major sections]

---

## Your Next Move / Key Takeaways

[Action-oriented conclusion with clear next steps]

If you're ready to [relevant CTA], reach out at **eytan@benzeno.com** or join the **REIGNation** community at **[https://reignation.com](https://reignation.com)**.

---

### Hashtags

#[Relevant] #[Hashtags] #[5-10 total]

---
```

**Content Guidelines:**
- 1500-2500 words
- Include relevant statistics with sources in parentheses
- Use bullet points and numbered lists where appropriate
- Bold key phrases for emphasis
- Include actionable insights ("Action tip:" callouts where relevant)
- End with a call-to-action
- Add 5-10 relevant hashtags at the end

**Relevant Tags (choose 1-3):**
- AI, Technology, Business, Real Estate, Energy, Science, Personal Development, Finance, Blockchain

### Step 3: Generate Hero Image

After writing the post, use DALL-E or describe a hero image that should be generated:
- Professional, modern aesthetic
- Relevant to the topic
- Would work well as a blog header (16:9 aspect ratio preferred)
- Clean, not too busy
- Colors that complement the eytan.com brand (purple/blue gradient theme)

Provide a detailed image prompt for generation.

### Step 4: Output Format

Provide the final output in this format:

```
## BLOG POST METADATA
- **Title:** [Compelling title]
- **Slug:** [url-friendly-slug]
- **Excerpt:** [2-3 sentence compelling excerpt for previews]
- **Tags:** [Array of 1-3 tags from the list above]
- **Author:** Eytan Benzeno
- **Icon:** [Relevant emoji]

## HERO IMAGE PROMPT
[Detailed prompt for image generation]

[Full markdown content of the blog post]
```
