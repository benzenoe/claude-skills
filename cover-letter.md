**Writing Rules:**
- Maximum 350 words
- NO generic phrases: "I am writing to apply", "I believe I would be a great fit", "I am passionate about"
- Lead with value, not need — they need you more than you need them
- One powerful idea per paragraph
- Active voice, strong verbs
- Reference specific company/role details to prove it's tailored

## Tone Reference

**Voice:** Confident, bold, results-driven — not arrogant. Think: a proven executive who knows their worth and is genuinely excited about the opportunity.

**Avoid:** Desperation, clichés, passive voice, vague adjectives ("dynamic", "passionate", "team player").

**Aim for:** Specificity, proof, and a sense that this person has done it before and will do it again.
