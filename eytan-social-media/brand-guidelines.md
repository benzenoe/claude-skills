## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### Overall Voice Characteristics
- **Knowledgeable but not condescending**
- **Direct and actionable**
- **Warm and engaging**
- **Data-informed**
- **Forward-thinking**

### Tone by Context

#### Educational Content
```
Tone: Expert guide
Example: "Here's the framework I use with clients to evaluate AI tools..."
Avoid: Jargon without explanation, overly technical language
```

#### News Commentary
```
Tone: Informed analyst
Example: "This announcement signals a major shift in how businesses will..."
Avoid: Sensationalism, unfounded predictions
```

#### Personal Stories
```
Tone: Relatable peer
Example: "I made this mistake early in my career, and here's what it taught me..."
Avoid: Humble-bragging, excessive self-promotion
```

#### Promotional Content
```
Tone: Confident consultant
Example: "If your business is struggling with [problem], here's how I can help..."
Avoid: Hard sells, desperation, generic claims
```

### Language Preferences

**Use:**
- "AI transformation" over "AI implementation"
- "Strategy" over "tactics"
- "Results" over "outcomes"
- "Businesses" or "companies" over "brands"
- "I've found..." or "In my experience..." for credibility

**Avoid:**
- Buzzwords without substance ("synergy," "leverage," "disrupt")
- Excessive emojis (1-3 max per post)
- ALL CAPS (except for emphasis in video text)
- Clickbait language ("You won't believe...")
- Negative language about competitors

### Multilingual Considerations

**English Posts:**
- Primary language for all platforms
- US English spelling (color, not colour)
- Clear, globally accessible vocabulary

**French Posts:**
- Use for Europe-focused content
- Formal "vous" form for professional contexts
- Maintain same level of expertise and warmth
- Tag with #French or #Fran√ßais for algorithm

**Carousel Cover:**

```
modern sans-serif typography placeholder,
subtle turquoise accent elements,
tech/AI themed iconography,
professional consultant branding,
1080x1350 pixels (Instagram carousel)
```

**TikTok/Reels Thumbnail:**
```
Vibrant turquoise background with bold contrast,
space for large white text overlay,
modern tech aesthetic,
eye-catching and scroll-stopping,
9:16 aspect ratio
```

### Image Dos and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Use brand colors consistently
- Include clean, modern backgrounds
- Feature tech-related imagery
- Maintain professional aesthetic
- Use high contrast for readability

**Don't:**
- Use stock photos of people (unless Eytan himself)
- Include competitors' logos/branding
- Use outdated tech imagery (old computers, etc.)
- Overload with text
- Use clashing colors outside the palette
