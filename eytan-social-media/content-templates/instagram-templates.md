## Instagram Best Practices Reminder

- **First Line:** Hook that stops the scroll
- **Format:** Use line breaks, don't wall of text
- **Hashtags:** 20-30, mix of sizes (some with millions, some with thousands)
- **Timing:** Monday-Friday, 11 AM-1 PM or 7-9 PM ET
- **Stories:** Post 3-5 per day for visibility
- **Reels:** Algorithm favors short-form video
- **Engagement:** Reply to every comment in first hour
- **Visuals:** Brand colors (teal/turquoise) in every image
