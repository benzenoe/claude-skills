## LinkedIn Best Practices Reminder

- **First Line:** Make it compelling - this appears in the feed preview
- **Length:** 1,000-1,500 characters performs best
- **Structure:** Use line breaks for readability
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 at the end
- **Timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM ET
- **Engagement:** Reply to comments within 2 hours of posting
