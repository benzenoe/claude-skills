## TikTok Best Practices Reminder

- **Hook:** First 3 seconds determine everything
- **Length:** 15-60 seconds performs best
- **Text:** Always add text overlays for accessibility
- **Audio:** Use trending sounds when appropriate
- **Captions:** Keep short, use relevant hashtags
- **Timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 7-9 PM ET
- **Frequency:** 1-2 videos per day ideal
- **Engagement:** Reply to comments with videos
- **Trends:** Participate in relevant trends quickly
- **Energy:** Higher energy than other platforms

## Video Quality Tips

- Good lighting (natural or ring light)
- Clean background or branded backdrop
- Clear audio (use lapel mic if needed)
- Stable footage (tripod or steady hand)
- Eye contact with camera
- Confident, energetic delivery
