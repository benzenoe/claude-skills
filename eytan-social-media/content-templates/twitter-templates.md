## X/Twitter Best Practices Reminder

- **Character Limit:** 280 (or Premium for longer)
- **Threads:** Number clearly (1/, 2/, 3/)
- **Hashtags:** 1-2 max, or none
- **Timing:** Weekdays, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM ET
- **Engagement:** Quote tweet with value, don't just retweet
- **Frequency:** 3-5 tweets per day ideal
- **Voice:** More casual, direct, punchy than LinkedIn
- **Threads:** Hook must be irresistible in tweet 1
